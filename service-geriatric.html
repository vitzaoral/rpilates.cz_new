Geriatric rehabilitation covers three areasnormal aging due to disuse cardiovascular problems like osteoarthritis conditions such as knee and hip replacements. The types of problems faced in geriatric physiotherapy are grouped into three different categories.

01

One category is the problems that happen because the patient simply does not use their limbs or does not exercise.

02

Another category geriatric rehabilitation deals with is cardiovascular disease, like heart disease and stroke.

03

The third category is skeletal problems. Geriatric rehabilitation helps people who have these disorders and osteoarthritis.
